Welcome to Kabul, Afghanistan

About Kabul

Kabul is the capital and largest city of Afghanistan. Located in the eastern half of the country, it is also a municipality, forming part of the Kabul Province. In 2025 its population is estimated to be 7.17 million. In contemporary times, Kabul has served as Afghanistan's political, cultural, and economic center. Rapid urbanization has made it the country's primate city and the 76th-largest city in the world.

History

Kabul has a long and complex history, dating back over 3,500 years. It has been a key city along the Silk Road and has seen the rise and fall of many empires, including the Persian, Mughal, and British empires.

Top Attractions

  • Babur Gardens: A beautiful historic garden built by Emperor Babur.
  • Darul Aman Palace: A grand palace with a rich history.
  • Kabul Museum: Home to ancient artifacts and Afghan history.
  • Paghman Gardens: A peaceful retreat outside the city.
